About GIKI Location

Nestled in the serene hills of Topi, Swabi, Pakistan, the Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology (GIKI) is renowned for its state-of-the-art campus and breathtaking natural surroundings. GIKI is easily accessible and located at the heart of academic excellence in the region.
